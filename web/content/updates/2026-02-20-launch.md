PaleoWaifu is a prehistoric animal gacha game. Collect creatures from across Earth's history, trade with other players, and build the ultimate collection.

## Gacha

Pull creatures using Fossils. Every creature has a rarity: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, or Legendary. A pity system guarantees you won't go too long without something good. Soft pity kicks in at 50 pulls, and at 90 you're guaranteed a Legendary.

New players start with 10 Fossils. Log in daily to earn 3 more.

## Collection

Every creature you pull goes into your collection. Browse your creatures, check their stats, and see what you're still missing.

## Trading

Want something another player has? Create a trade offer on the marketplace. You can propose trades, browse open offers, and build your collection through the community.

## Encyclopedia

All creatures in the game are listed in the encyclopedia. Filter by rarity, type, era, or diet to find what you're looking for, even before you've pulled it.

## Leaderboards

See who's on top. Leaderboards rank players by total XP earned and by collection size. Earn XP through pulls, trades, daily logins, and chatting in the Discord server.

## Discord Bot

PaleoWaifu has a Discord bot. Pull creatures, claim dailies, check your balance, and more, all from Discord. Chatting in a server with the bot earns XP toward your level.
