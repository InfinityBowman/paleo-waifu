Welcome to **PaleoWaifu** — the prehistoric animal waifu gacha game!

### What's included

- **Gacha system** with 5 rarities: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, and Legendary
- **Pity system** — soft pity at 50, guaranteed legendary at 90
- **Daily fossils** — log in every day for free pulls
- **Trading** — trade creatures with other players
- **Encyclopedia** — browse all creatures
- **Leaderboards** — compete for top XP and collection size
