Your creatures can now fight. Build a team, climb the ranks, and prove your collection is the strongest.

## Arena & Friendly Battles

Two ways to battle:

- **Arena** — Ranked PvP. Win to gain rating, lose to drop. You get 5 arena attacks per day, so make them count.
- **Friendly** — Search for any player and battle them with no rating on the line. Great for testing teams.

When you attack in the arena, your team fights the opponent's **defense team** controlled by AI. Set both an offense and defense team from the Teams tab — your defense team is what others fight when they challenge you.

## Team Building

Each team is 3 creatures. No duplicate species allowed.

Every creature has a **role** that determines its stat spread:

- **Striker** — High ATK, built to deal damage
- **Tank** — High HP and DEF, absorbs hits for the team
- **Support** — Heals and buffs allies
- **Bruiser** — Balanced stats, flexible in any position

Place creatures in the **front** or **back** row. Front row creatures are targeted first by single-target attacks, and some abilities get bonuses from row positioning.

### Synergies

Matching creatures by **type**, **era**, or **diet** grants team-wide stat bonuses:

- **3 of the same type**: +7% HP, +3% ATK
- **3 from the same era**: +3% all stats
- **All carnivores**: +10% ATK, +7% SPD
- **All herbivores**: +10% DEF, +10% HP
- **Mixed diet**: +12% SPD, +7% ATK

Synergies stack, so team composition matters.

## Abilities

Each creature has two ability slots:

- **Active** — Choose when to use it. Has a cooldown between uses.
- **Passive** — Triggers automatically based on conditions.

There are 15 active and 10+ passive abilities across damage, healing, buffs, debuffs, shields, and crowd control. Some highlights:

- **Crushing Jaw** — Heavy single-target damage
- **Tail Sweep** — Hit all enemies at once
- **Rally Cry** — Boost your whole team's ATK and SPD
- **Shield Wall** — Grant a shield to absorb incoming damage
- **Headbutt** — Stun an enemy, forcing them to skip a turn
- **Venom Strike** — Deal damage and apply poison over time

When no active ability is ready, creatures use a basic attack.

## Combat

Battles play out over up to 30 turns. Turn order is based on SPD each round.

**Damage formula** factors in ATK, DEF mitigation, a 10% crit chance (1.5x damage), and a dodge chance that scales with the defender's SPD advantage. Status effects like poison, bleed, buffs, and debuffs tick each turn.

If no team is wiped by turn 30, the team with higher remaining HP% wins. Ties go to the defender.

## Rating & Tiers

Everyone starts at a base rating and climbs through five tiers:

| Tier    | Rating    |
| ------- | --------- |
| Bronze  | 0–499     |
| Silver  | 500–999   |
| Gold    | 1000–1499 |
| Diamond | 1500–1999 |
| Apex    | 2000+     |

Wins grant **+25** rating, losses cost **-20**. Matchmaking pairs you against players within a similar rating range.

## Battle Replays

Every battle is saved. View the full turn-by-turn log from the History tab — see ability usage, damage numbers, crits, dodges, status effects, and KOs as they happened.
